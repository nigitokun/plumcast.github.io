Podcast Topics

ACADEMICS

Need some insights about your study?

Academic podcasting is a brand-new method of content production, a vehicle for the dissemination of knowledge, and a component of a movement that aims to increase intellectual engagement.

Additionally, podcasting in academia has the power to create new professional groups, open up access to knowledge, and alter how people think about research. 

MENTAL HEALTH

Your mental health matters.

Plumcast promotes mental health programs in order to create a safe, stigma-free workplace where everyone feels like they belong. Plumcast's key objectives are to normalize conversation, promote professional and self-care, and raise awareness and knowledge. on issues like kindness, toughness, rest, and anxiousness. We believe that when we help one another, we improve.

About us

Janella Famor Mina — FOUNDER

Plumcast began as a startup in Metro Manila, Philippines. Founders developed the software with a simple goal of creating a platform for podcast creators and listeners. Plumcast offers a free service with ads as well as an ad-free, subscription-based service, making it easy for listeners to access their podcasts. The algorithms make a satisfying listening experience that is customized to each users. Plumcast also keeps it simple with the ability to download episodes from the streaming platform onto your device and add bookmarks to your favorites.
